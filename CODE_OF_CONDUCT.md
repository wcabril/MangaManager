# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This is a small open-source project. Everyone who participates — by opening issues, submitting pull requests, or leaving comments — is expected to be respectful and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive comments
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Violations may result in comments being removed or the contributor being blocked from the repository.

## Contact

If you have concerns, open an issue or reach out via [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/wcabril).
